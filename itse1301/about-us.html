About Me

My name is Nick, I grew up in the DFW area and now live in Dallas while working at Parkland Hospital. I've been an x-ray tech for over a decade and I'm ready for a change and to challenge myself to learn something new. I enjoy doing artistic and creative hobbies, gardening, remodeling around the house, and exploring the great outdoors. I am quick to learn new skills with a desire to learn how things work. I hope to test my creative and problem solving skills while beginning a new career that is equally, rewarding, and allows me to follow dreams of becoming a starving artist.
